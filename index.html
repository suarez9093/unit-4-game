Cereal Collector


Instrunctions
Click on your favorite cereal characters below to start playing!

1. By clicking on a character image below you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

2. The value of each character is hidden until you click on it and then is updated in your total score

3. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number that is generated. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each character.
